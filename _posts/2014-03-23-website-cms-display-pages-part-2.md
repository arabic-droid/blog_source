Page Design
===========

In his awesome book, "Don't Make Me Think"&nbsp;<a href="http://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/0321965515/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1634&amp;creative=6738&amp;creativeASIN=0321965515&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=mindlramblnon-21" target="_blank">(shameless affiliate link)</a>,&nbsp;Steve Krug drives home the message that time spent figuring out how your site is supposed to work is not time spent deciding to engage with your site. So, we're not going to do any ground-breaking design work for this company web page.

<!--more-->

When people visit the site they should understand straight away how they're supposed to use it. An image search for <a href="https://www.google.co.uk/search?q=company+website&amp;safe=off&amp;espv=210&amp;es_sm=119&amp;source=lnms&amp;tbm=isch&amp;sa=X&amp;ei=dNwmU8XBKu6y0AW144GgBQ&amp;ved=0CAkQ_AUoAQ&amp;biw=1246&amp;bih=658#pws=0&amp;q=company+website&amp;safe=off&amp;tbm=isch" target="_blank">'company website'</a>&nbsp;shows basically the same design over and over again - and, I expect, you'll be instantly familiar with it.</div><div><br /></div><div>A logo, a navigation bar, a large carousel or image area, some content in columns below, and a footer.</div><div><br /></div><table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody><tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://fc06.deviantart.net/fs70/f/2011/136/f/4/cargo_company_website_layout__by_rizmax-d3ghbws.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><img border="0" src="http://fc06.deviantart.net/fs70/f/2011/136/f/4/cargo_company_website_layout__by_rizmax-d3ghbws.jpg" height="320" width="278" /></a></td></tr><tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">A random image from Google images search for 'company website' (not an endorsement)</td></tr></tbody></table><div>There are relatively few company websites that step away from this basic design.
